The Competition:

What's the market for coding like?

Overall, the market for coding is highly diverse and offers no shortage of options, both paid and plenty of free ones too. Everything from FreeCodeCamp to MIT OpenCourseWare is available for coders of all skill levels, attention spans, and walks of life (though some courses are more rigorous than others). This makes it difficult to recap the industry as a whole, but it is a dynamic, innovative, and highly technical one with its own set of cues and preferences. Bright and futuristic colors, more geometric page layouts, and more robotic fonts that lack stylization or curly, more antiquated characters set these sites and creators apart, which jives well with the fields they specialize in and want to impart to their audience.
